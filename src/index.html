A Toy For Every Dog and Every Home

The Odin's minimal, modern design is inspired by geometric shapes and art. It took months of prototyping to create a toy we loved as much as our dog did.

It features integrated flaps to easily place treats in, differently sized holes allow treats of varying sizes to fall out and is big enough to not be swallowed by big dogs but light enough for small dogs.

FAQ

How tough is The Odin?

Our dog is a chewer so we designed The Odin to withstand his strong bite and have tested The Odin with dogs of all sizes. We selected a material called TPE/TPR, which is a food safe plastic because Odin's most durable toys use that material as well.

The Odin is not a chew toy though. Supervise and guide your dog as they play. Once they start chewing, take it away and toss it for them to fetch and play again.

What's the benefit of having 2 or more Odin toys?

Connecting additional toys keeps The Odin interesting and challenging, making your dog play with it longer. When just one Odin is used, it rolls around in all directions. When two or more Odins are connected, it does not roll as easily and is harder to pick up so your dog will have to play with it differently.

Every dog we've tested with plays with one toy much more differently than they do with two toys connected since it changes from a ball to a longer log-like shape when two are attached together.

What's the dimensions of The Odin?

3.6in x 3.3in x 3.8in, bigger than a tennis ball but smaller than a softball. It holds about 1 cup food.

What's your warranty/return policy?

About

Big ears and big heart

Ever since our dog, Odin, came into our lives, he’s shared his innate happiness with everyone who encounters him. The joy he so easily spreads inspired us to start Up Dog Toys with a mission to make happy dogs and happy dog owners.
